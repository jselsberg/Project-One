About Me

Hi everyone! I am a senior at Washington University in St. Louis majoring in Philosophy-Neuroscience-Psychology. When I am not learning about the brain, watching a movie, or playing sports with friends, I enjoy taking pictures of interesting events and locations.

My passion for pictures began about a year ago. I studied abroad in Edinburgh, Scotland for six months, which inspired me to capture famous landmarks and cultural scenes in unique ways. I only take the highest-quality pictures with my iPhone 4S camera, which is only about a year old.
